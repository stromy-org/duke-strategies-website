# Brand Voice — Translation Guide

## Archetype & Tone

- Archetype: ruler / luxury (from charter.json)
- Tone: confident, editorial, understated, authoritative, precise
- Register: formal `u`, corporate-professional, never casual

## Signature Metaphors

- **Bridges / architecture / crossings** — central to Duke's visual
  identity (Erasmusbrug, Nesciobrug, Pythonbrug). When the EN copy
  uses "bridge" or "bridging" as a metaphor, translate literally
  ("brug", "overbruggen") — it is idiomatic in Dutch. If a specific
  bridge metaphor doesn't work in NL, rephrase to keep architectural
  vocabulary.
- **Stakeholder landscape** — use "stakeholderlandschap" (compound
  noun, standard in NL corporate vocabulary).
- **Stakeholder-proof** — use "stakeholder-proof" (English loanword
  accepted in NL professional context, per glossary).

## Sentence Rhythm

- Editorial, measured sentences. No staccato marketing copy.
- Prefer compound sentences with semicolons over bullet-point phrasing.
- Understated confidence — let facts carry authority, not adjectives.
- NL may be ~10% longer than EN — accept this, do not compress meaning.

## Do-Not Patterns

- No exclamation marks in professional copy (ever)
- No Dutch equivalents of "awesome", "amazing", "incredible"
- No anglicisms beyond the glossary whitelist
- No diminutives (-je / -tje) in professional context
- No marketing superlatives ("de allerbeste", "uniek in de markt")

## Few-Shot Anchors (EN → NL)

### Example 1 — Tagline
- EN: "Bridging Strategy and Impact"
- NL: "Bridging Strategy and Impact" (kept in English per glossary)

### Example 2 — Bio sentence
- EN: "Ingo Heijnen is Managing Partner and co-founder of Duke
  Strategies with 27 years of experience in strategic communications
  and public affairs."
- NL: "Ingo Heijnen is Managing Partner en medeoprichter van Duke
  Strategies met 27 jaar ervaring in strategische communicatie en
  public affairs."

### Example 3 — Service description
- EN: "High-level strategic counsel for boards, executives, and
  leadership teams navigating complex corporate, political, and
  societal challenges."
- NL: "Strategisch advies op het hoogste niveau voor bestuurders,
  directies en leiderschapsteams die complexe bedrijfsmatige,
  politieke en maatschappelijke uitdagingen navigeren."

### Example 4 — Philosophy copy
- EN: "We guide companies and their leaders through the complex
  political, social and business environment of stakeholder
  perceptions and opinions."
- NL: "Wij begeleiden bedrijven en hun leiders door het complexe
  politieke, sociale en bedrijfsmatige landschap van stakeholder-
  percepties en -opinies."
