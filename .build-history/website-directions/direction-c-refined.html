Building Bridges

"We believe that by successfully bridging the gap between business realities and diverging stakeholder expectations, corporations can deliver more value for all."

Duke Strategies is a corporate advisory firm that helps organisations navigate the complex intersection of business strategy and stakeholder relations. Our multidisciplinary team combines decades of experience in corporate strategy, public affairs, crisis management, and communications.

We work with boards, executives, and leadership teams to develop strategies that are not only commercially sound but also resilient to the scrutiny of an increasingly demanding stakeholder landscape — from regulators and investors to media, employees, and the public.

Our mission is straightforward: help organisations build lasting legitimacy by aligning what they do with what their stakeholders expect. We call this making strategy stakeholder proof.

Whether you are preparing for a major transaction, navigating a crisis, or rethinking your corporate positioning, Duke Strategies provides the intellectual rigour and practical expertise to move forward with confidence.

Our Method

The Approach

01

Analyse

We map the full stakeholder ecosystem and assess the strategic landscape. Through rigorous analysis of political, regulatory, media, and market dynamics, we identify where risks and opportunities converge — giving leadership a clear picture of the forces that will shape their decisions.

02

Create

We develop integrated strategies that bridge commercial objectives with stakeholder expectations. This means crafting narratives, positioning frameworks, and engagement plans that are intellectually coherent, politically astute, and commercially viable — strategies that hold up under pressure.

03

Execute

We stand alongside our clients in execution — managing stakeholder engagement, media relations, and crisis response in real time. Our team operates with discretion and precision, ensuring that strategy translates into measurable outcomes and lasting relationships.

Capabilities

What We Do

Strategic Advisory

Board-level counsel on corporate strategy, positioning, and stakeholder alignment. We help leadership teams make decisions that withstand public scrutiny and regulatory challenge.

Crisis Communications

Rapid-response crisis management combining strategic counsel with hands-on execution. We protect reputations, manage narratives, and restore stakeholder confidence under pressure.

Stakeholder Mapping & Engagement

Systematic identification and engagement of key stakeholders — investors, regulators, media, employees, and communities — to build coalitions of support.

M&A Communications

Strategic communications advisory for mergers, acquisitions, and corporate transactions. We manage the narrative from announcement through integration.

Media Training & Positioning

Executive media preparation combining message development with intensive interview training. We ensure spokespeople communicate with clarity, authority, and discipline.

Issue Management

Proactive identification and management of emerging issues before they become crises. We monitor, assess, and develop response strategies for complex regulatory and reputational challenges.
