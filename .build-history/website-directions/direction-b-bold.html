WHO
WE ARE

Duke Strategies is a consultancy that operates on the intersection of corporate strategy and corporate affairs and helps to build bridges between business leaders and their stakeholders.

In an era of unprecedented complexity, the companies that thrive are those that align their strategic ambitions with the expectations of regulators, investors, employees, and the public. We exist to make that alignment happen.

Our approach is rooted in data-driven analysis, deep stakeholder mapping, and decades of experience navigating high-stakes corporate environments. We don't just advise — we architect the bridge between where you are and where you need to be.
