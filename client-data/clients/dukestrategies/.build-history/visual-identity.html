03 — Logo System

Logo Lockups

The Duke Strategies logo features a coral rectangle housing the geometric bridge-pillar mark with "Duke" in white, and "strategies" set below in coral. Three variants cover all background scenarios.

On Dark
On White
On Coral (White Variant)
On Light Grey (Grey Variant)

Size & Spacing Guidelines

Maximum Width
120pt
The logo should never exceed 120pt (160px) in width on print or screen materials.
Clear Space
0.5x
Maintain a minimum clear space equal to half the logo mark height on all sides.
Minimum Size
32px
The logo must never be rendered smaller than 32px in height to ensure legibility.
